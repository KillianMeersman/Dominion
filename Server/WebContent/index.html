Welcome to Dominion!

To start the tutorial click anywhere.

To go to the next step you can use the arrow keys or simply click anywhere.

Action Cards

These are the special cards you can buy who have all special effects.

You can click on the card to view that specific card in spectator mode or you can click on the "+"-button (if you can afford it) to buy that card.

Victory Cards

These are cards that you can buy to gain victory-points which you need to win the game.

You can click on the card to view that specific card in spectator mode or you can click on the "+"-button (if you can afford it) to buy that card.

Treasure Cards

These are cards that you can buy to gain gain gold when you draw a treasure card to your hand. Gold can be used to buy more cards!

You can click on the card to view that specific card in spectator mode or you can click on the "+"-button (if you can afford it) to buy that card.

Curse Card

This is a card that you don't normally want to buy.

The curse cards gives you -1 victory points so it is best to combo this card with specific action cards. The witch card also gives an opponent a curse card.

End Turn and Play Treasure button

The "End Turn"-button if pressed will end your turn so the next player can make his moves!

The "Play Treasure"-button is handy when you have more than 2 treasure cards in your hand and you want to play them all in once.

Thrash pile

In the thrash pile are all the cards that are permantally discarded. You can click on the pile to view those cards.

Amount of Actions/Buys/Coins

This will display how many actions, buys and coins you have left.

Player's Hand

This is the current player's hand where you can view all the cards that you can play this turn.

To view a card just simply hover over a card or to play a card just click on that desired card.

Current Player

Displays the name of who's turn it currently is.

Discard Pile and Deck

The upper pile is the discard pile. All the cards who are played and will return to your deck in the next cicle will end up here.

The lower pile is your deck. At each start of your turn you will automatically draw cards from here.

The player stats

On this pannel you can view your opponents name and the first card on their discard pile. Click to dropdown.
